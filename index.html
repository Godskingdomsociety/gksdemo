Who We Are

A Fellowship Built
on the Living Word

God's Kingdom Society (GKS) is a worldwide Christian fellowship founded on the uncompromising truth of God's Holy Word. We are a people called to worship in spirit and in truth, to study the Scriptures diligently, and to live as ambassadors of Christ's coming Kingdom.

With branches spread across Nigeria and the Diaspora, GKS stands as a beacon of sound doctrine, fervent prayer, and genuine fellowship — welcoming all who hunger for the authentic Christian life.

Meet Our Leadership

We hold fast to the whole counsel of God's Word without addition or subtraction.
