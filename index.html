Who We Are

A Fellowship Built
on the Living Word

The God's Kingdom Society is purely a Christian Organisation founded by JEHOVAH, the Almighty God through the instrumentality of Saint Gideon Meriodere Urhobo, of blessed memory — the instrument chosen by God through Jesus Christ to accomplish His purpose.

"Proclaiming the Kingdom of God with uncompromising truth since 1934."

With branches spread across Nigeria and the Diaspora, GKS stands as a beacon of sound doctrine, fervent prayer, and genuine fellowship — welcoming all who hunger for the authentic Christian life.
